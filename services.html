FAQ

How do appointments work?

All services are by appointment only. We offer three options: mobile on-site repair, pickup & drop-off, or scheduled drop-offs. We’ll confirm a time window and the best service option based on your issue.

How do parts work?

We use high-quality parts and we always confirm with you before purchasing or installing anything. If we discover additional parts are needed during the repair, we’ll contact you with options and pricing.

What if I don’t want to proceed after diagnosis?

If you choose not to proceed after we diagnose the issue, you’ll only be charged for the completed portion of work (for example: diagnosis, labour already performed, or any agreed call-out). We’ll keep it fair and clear.

Do you work on e-bikes?

We can handle many common mechanical services on e-bikes (brakes, tyres, drivetrain). For electrical issues or proprietary systems, we’ll advise the best next step.
